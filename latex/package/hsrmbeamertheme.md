## Summary

```md
**hsrmbeamertheme**, a specialized Beamer theme, allows users to create presentations that adhere to the corporate design of RheinMain University of Applied Sciences. It provides a streamlined way for students and faculty to maintain branding consistency in their presentations. This theme is particularly useful for academic settings, ensuring slides not only look professional but also align with university guidelines. The repository includes the theme's styling files and background templates, making it easier to implement in LaTeX projects for presentations.

Tags: `LaTeX`, `Beamer`, `presentation`, `theme`, `RheinMain`
```
