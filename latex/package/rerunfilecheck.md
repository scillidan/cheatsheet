## Summary

```md
`rerunfilecheck`, a LaTeX package, helps automate the checking of auxiliary files during the document compilation process. This tool streamlines the workflow by ensuring that the necessary references and files are updated without the need for constant manual re-checking. It is particularly useful for researchers and writers who generate complex documents with multiple dependencies, making the compilation more efficient and less error-prone.

Tags: `LaTeX`, `automation`, `auxiliary`, `compilation`, `package`
```
