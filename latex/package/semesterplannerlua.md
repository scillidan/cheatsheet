## Summary

```md
**semesterplannerLua**, a LaTeX package, is designed to help users create organized semester plans by allowing them to print timetables, lists of appointments, and exams. It includes functionalities to draw customizable calendars tailored to specific needs, making it a practical tool for students and educators alike.

This project simplifies semester planning and enhances productivity by providing the necessary features to manage academic schedules efficiently. It's especially useful for those who prefer a structured approach to their studies or require a visual representation of their commitments.

Tags: `LaTeX`, `semester`, `timetable`, `calendar`, `planning`
```
