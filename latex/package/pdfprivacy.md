## Summary

```md
The repository `pdfprivacy` is a LaTeX package designed to remove or suppress PDF meta-data. It enables users to enhance the privacy of their PDF documents generated through LaTeX by clearing specific meta-data fields. This tool is particularly useful for researchers and authors who wish to safeguard their personal information, ensuring that sensitive data does not accompany their published works. The package allows a seamless integration into LaTeX workflows, providing a simple solution for meta-data management.

Tags: `LaTeX`, `PDF`, `privacy`, `meta-data`, `document management`
```
