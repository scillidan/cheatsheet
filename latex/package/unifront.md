## Summary

```md
`unifront`, a LaTeX package, simplifies the formatting of notes by providing a unique front page for each chapter and section. It helps users create a structured layout and keeps track of their progress throughout their notes. This tool is particularly useful for students and professionals who aim to improve the organization and presentation of their documents. The repository includes the package source, documentation, and examples to facilitate its use.

Tags: `latex`, `notes`, `front-page`, `formatting`, `documentation`
```
