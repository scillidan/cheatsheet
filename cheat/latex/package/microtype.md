## Summary

```md
**Microtype**, a LaTeX package, enhances the typography of documents by improving the spacing and appearance of text. It provides various typographic refinements, such as character protrusion and font expansion, thereby enhancing the legibility and aesthetic quality of printed materials. This package is particularly useful for those who want to achieve a polished look in their LaTeX documents without extensive manual adjustment.

Tags: `LaTeX`, `typography`, `microtype`, `document`, `text-spacing`
```

**Microtype** 是一个 LaTeX 包，通过改善文本的间距和外观来增强文档的排版效果。它提供多种排版细节的改进，例如字符突出和字体伸展，从而提高印刷材料的可读性和美观性。对于那些希望在 LaTeX 文档中实现精致外观而无需进行大量手动调整的用户，这个包特别有用。
