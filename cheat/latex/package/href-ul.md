## Summary

```md
`href-ul`, a LaTeX package, enhances the appearance of hyperlinks by making them underlined. This package is designed for users who want their documents to mimic the underlined hyperlinks found on the web, providing a more visually appealing and consistent style. To use it, you need to install it from CTAN and include it in the preamble of your LaTeX documents. The project also relies on the `hyperref` and `ulem` packages, making it easy to integrate into classic LaTeX setups.

Tags: `LaTeX`, `hyperlinks`, `underlined`, `hyperref`, `ulem`
```
