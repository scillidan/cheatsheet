## Summary

```md
`tex4ebook`, a conversion tool, facilitates the transformation of LaTeX documents into various ebook formats such as EPUB and MOBI. It utilizes the TeX4ht framework combined with texlua scripts, making it easy for users to leverage their existing LaTeX documents for digital publication. This tool is particularly useful for authors and researchers who wish to distribute their work electronically without losing formatting or content integrity. It streamlines the process of converting academic or professional documents into widely-used ebook formats.

Tags: `LaTeX`, `ebook`, `EPUB`, `MOBI`, `Tex4ht`
```
