How to store batata?

```md
Storing batata (sweet potatoes) properly is essential for maintaining their quality, flavor, and nutritional value. Here are some effective tips for storing batata:

### 1. **Cool, Dry Place**:
   - **Temperature**: Store sweet potatoes in a cool, dark place with a temperature ideally between 55°F to 60°F (13°C to 16°C). A pantry, basement, or cupboard works well.
   - **Avoid Refrigeration**: Do not refrigerate sweet potatoes, as cold temperatures can cause their starches to convert to sugars, resulting in a gritty texture and an unpleasant sweet flavor.

### 2. **Good Air Circulation**:
   - **Containers**: Place sweet potatoes in a well-ventilated container such as a basket, wooden crate, or cardboard box. Avoid plastic bags or containers that are airtight since they can trap moisture and lead to spoilage.
   - **Separate Layers**: If stacking sweet potatoes, ensure they are in a single layer or with minimal stacking to avoid bruising.

### 3. **Avoid Sunlight and Moisture**:
   - **Darkness**: Keep sweet potatoes away from direct sunlight, as exposure can lead to sprouting and spoilage.
   - **Humidity**: Store them in a dry environment. Excess moisture can cause rot or mold growth, so ensure the storage area is not humid.

### 4. **Regular Checks**:
   - **Inspect for Spoilage**: Periodically check your sweet potatoes for signs of spoilage, such as soft spots, mold, or sprouting. Remove any affected ones immediately to prevent them from affecting the others.

### 5. **Long-Term Storage**:
   - **Curing**: If you have freshly harvested sweet potatoes, consider curing them first. Curing involves keeping them in a warm, humid environment (around 80°F or 27°C) for about 10-14 days. This process helps to heal any minor wounds and enhances their sweetness.

### 6. **Freezing**:
   - If you wish to store sweet potatoes for an extended period, consider cooking and then freezing them. Baked, mashed, or roasted sweet potatoes can be frozen in airtight containers for up to 6 months.

By following these guidelines, you can help ensure your batata stays fresh and flavorful for as long as possible.
```

```md
妥善储存甘薯（甜土豆）对于保持其质量、风味和营养价值至关重要。以下是一些有效的甘薯储存技巧：

### 1. **阴凉、干燥的地方**：
   - **温度**：将甘薯储存在阴凉、黑暗的地方，最佳温度为55°F到60°F（13°C到16°C）。储藏室、地下室或橱柜都非常合适。
   - **避免冷藏**：不要将甘薯放入冰箱，因为低温会导致其淀粉转化为糖，造成粗糙的口感和不愉快的甜味。

### 2. **良好的空气流通**：
   - **容器**：将甘薯放在通风良好的容器中，比如篮子、木箱或纸箱。避免使用塑料袋或密封容器，因为它们会困住水分，导致腐烂。
   - **分层存放**：如果需要叠放甘薯，确保是一层放置或最小叠加，以避免碰伤。

### 3. **避免阳光和湿气**：
   - **黑暗**：将甘薯放在避免直射阳光的地方，阳光照射可能导致发芽和腐烂。
   - **湿度**：将其存放在干燥的环境中。过多的水分会导致腐烂或霉菌生长，所以确保储存区域不潮湿。

### 4. **定期检查**：
   - **检查腐烂情况**：定期检查甘薯是否有腐烂的迹象，例如软斑、霉菌或发芽。如发现受影响的甘薯，请立即将其移除，以防影响其他甘薯。

### 5. **长期储存**：
   - **治愈**：如果您有新采摘的甘薯，考虑先进行治愈。治愈是指将甘薯放在温暖、湿润的环境中（约80°F或27°C）保持10-14天。这一过程有助于愈合小伤口，并增强其甜味。

### 6. **冷冻**：
   - 如果希望将甘薯存放更长时间，可以考虑先烹饪然后冷冻。烘烤、捣碎或烤制的甘薯可以放在密封容器中冷冻，保鲜期可达6个月。

通过遵循这些指导方针，您可以帮助确保甘薯保持尽可能新鲜和美味。
```
