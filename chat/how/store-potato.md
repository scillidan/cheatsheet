How to store potato?

```md
Storing potatoes correctly is essential to keep them fresh and prevent spoilage. Here are some effective methods for storing potatoes:

### 1. **Choose the Right Location**:
   - **Cool, Dark, and Well-Ventilated Area**: Store potatoes in a cool (ideally 45-50°F or 7-10°C), dark place to prevent sprouting and decay. A basement, pantry, or cupboard is typically ideal.
   - **Avoid Sunlight**: Exposure to light can cause potatoes to turn green and produce solanine, a toxic compound.

### 2. **Use the Right Containers**:
   - **Breathable Bags or Boxes**: Store potatoes in burlap sacks, mesh bags, or cardboard boxes that allow air circulation. Avoid using plastic bags, as they can trap moisture and lead to rot.
   - **Avoid Stacking**: If possible, avoid stacking potatoes too high, as this can cause bruising and lead to quicker spoilage.

### 3. **Keep Away from Other Produce**:
   - Potatoes should be stored away from fruits, especially apples, as they produce ethylene gas, which can speed up spoilage in potatoes.

### 4. **Check for Spoilage**:
   - Regularly check stored potatoes for any signs of spoilage, such as soft spots, sprouting, or mold. Remove any affected potatoes to prevent them from affecting the others.

### 5. **Limit Moisture**:
   - **Dryness**: Ensure potatoes are dry when storing. Excess moisture can promote mold and decay.
   - **Ventilation**: Use a container that allows for ventilation to reduce humidity.

### 6. **Long-Term Storage**:
   - For long-term storage, consider curing your potatoes after harvesting (if applicable) by keeping them in a warm, dark place for a couple of weeks to toughen the skin.

### Additional Tips:
- **Do Not Refrigerate**: Storing potatoes in the refrigerator can convert the starches into sugars, leading to an undesirable sweet flavor and an unpleasant texture when cooked.
- **Monitor Temperature**: If you live in a warm climate or experience fluctuations in temperature, consider using a dedicated storage area, such as a wine cellar or root cellar, if possible.

By following these guidelines, you can help ensure your potatoes stay fresh and usable for a longer time.
```

正确存放土豆对于保持其新鲜和防止腐烂至关重要。以下是一些有效的土豆存储方法：

### 1. **选择合适的位置**：
   - **阴凉、黑暗和通风良好的地方**：将土豆存放在阴凉（理想温度为45-50°F或7-10°C）、黑暗的地方，以防发芽和腐烂。地下室、食品储藏室或橱柜通常是理想选择。
   - **避免阳光直射**：暴露在光下会使土豆变绿，并产生一种有毒化合物—龙葵素。

### 2. **使用合适的容器**：
   - **透气的袋子或盒子**：将土豆存放在麻袋、网袋或可以透气的纸箱中，以促进空气流通。避免使用塑料袋，因为它们容易积聚水分并导致腐烂。
   - **避免堆叠**：如果可能，避免将土豆堆得太高，以免造成碰伤并导致更快腐坏。

### 3. **远离其他农产品**：
   - 土豆应与水果，尤其是苹果隔开储存，因为苹果会释放乙烯气体，从而加速土豆的腐烂。

### 4. **检查腐烂情况**：
   - 定期检查存放的土豆，观察是否有软点、发芽或霉变等腐烂迹象。移除任何受影响的土豆，以防它们影响其他土豆。

### 5. **限制湿气**：
   - **保持干燥**：确保土豆在存放时是干燥的。过多的水分会促进霉菌和腐烂。
   - **通风**：使用可以通风的容器以减少湿度。

### 6. **长期储存**：
   - 对于长期储存，考虑在采收后对土豆进行固化（如果适用），将其放在温暖、黑暗的地方保养几周，以增强表皮的坚韧性。

### 其他建议：
- **不要冷藏**：将土豆存放在冰箱中会将淀粉转化为糖，导致不理想的甜味和烹饪时的不愉快口感。
- **监控温度**：如果您居住在温暖的气候或温度波动较大，可考虑使用专门的储存区域，如酒窖或根窖（如有可能）。

通过遵循这些指南，您可以帮助确保土豆保持新鲜并可以长期使用。
